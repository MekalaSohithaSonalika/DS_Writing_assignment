Global Coal Consumption Trends

Coal consumption grew by 1.8% in 2024, marking the third consecutive year of growth. This increase was primarily driven by emerging economies in Asia, particularly for electricity generation and industrial use.

Key Developments

  • China accounted for 54% of global coal consumption
  • Retirements of coal plants in OECD countries reached record levels
  • Coal prices averaged $120/tonne, down from 2022 peaks
  • Carbon capture projects at coal plants increased by 40%
